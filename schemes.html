Healthcare Schemes

Government and private healthcare assistance programs to help you access quality healthcare

Government

Ayushman Bharat

National health protection scheme covering healthcare costs up to ₹5 lakh per family annually for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.

Eligibility: Low-income families
Government

CGHS

Central Government Health Scheme providing comprehensive medical care to central government employees, pensioners and their dependents.

Eligibility: Government employees
Government

PM-JAY

Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana offering health insurance coverage of up to ₹5 lakh per family for secondary and tertiary care.

Eligibility: Economically vulnerable
Government

ESI Scheme

Employee State Insurance providing socio-economic protection including medical care to workers and their families in the organized sector.

Eligibility: Organized sector workers
Government

RSBY

Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana providing health insurance coverage for BPL families for hospitalization expenses up to ₹30,000 per annum.

Eligibility: BPL families
Private

Private Health Insurance

Various private health insurance plans providing coverage for hospitalization, critical illness, and other medical expenses.

Eligibility: Anyone

Need Help Applying for These Schemes?

Our team can assist you in determining your eligibility and completing the application process for various healthcare schemes.

Many people miss out on beneficial healthcare programs due to lack of awareness or difficulty navigating the application process. We're here to help make healthcare more accessible for you and your family.
