Ayushman Bharat Government

National Health Protection Scheme providing health coverage to economically vulnerable families

About Ayushman Bharat

Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY) is the largest government-funded healthcare program in the world, aimed at providing free access to healthcare for low-income people in India. The scheme covers secondary and tertiary care hospitalization expenses of up to ₹5 lakh per family per year.

Key Features

  • Coverage of up to ₹5 lakh per family per year
  • Cashless and paperless access to quality healthcare services
  • Covers pre and post-hospitalization expenses
  • No restrictions on family size, age, or gender
  • Covers all pre-existing conditions from day one
  • Portable across all empanelled hospitals in India
  • 1,350+ medical packages covering surgery, medical treatment, diagnostics, and day care treatments

Eligibility Criteria

Families included in the SECC (Socio-Economic Caste Census) database are eligible for the scheme. The general criteria include:

  • Families listed in the SECC database with identified deprivations
  • Currently entitled rural families
  • Urban worker families as per defined criteria
  • No cap on family size and age of members

How to Apply

To benefit from the Ayushman Bharat scheme, eligible candidates need to follow these steps:

  1. Check eligibility online or through nearest Common Service Center
  2. Visit the nearest Ayushman Bharat empanelled hospital with required identification
  3. Complete verification process through Aadhaar or other acceptable ID proof
  4. Get the Ayushman Card generated
  5. Access healthcare services at any empanelled hospital across India

Covered Treatments

Ayushman Bharat covers a wide range of medical and surgical procedures including:

Medical Specialties
  • General Medicine
  • Cardiology
  • Neurology
  • Oncology
  • Pulmonology
  • Nephrology
  • Gastroenterology
  • Pediatrics
  • Infectious Diseases
Surgical Procedures
  • Cardiothoracic Surgery
  • Neurosurgery
  • Ophthalmology
  • Orthopedics
  • ENT Procedures
  • General Surgery
  • Gynecology
  • Urology
  • Plastic Surgery (for burns and injuries)

Empanelled Hospitals

The scheme has empanelled thousands of public and private hospitals across India. Beneficiaries can avail treatment at any of these hospitals. All empanelled hospitals display the Ayushman Bharat logo for easy identification.

To find empanelled hospitals near you, visit the official Ayushman Bharat website or call the toll-free helpline at 14555.
